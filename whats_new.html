What's New?

This being the third year of updates to this course, I wanted to add a little page here to highlight some of the updates, changes and additions made from previous years. This is predominantly for returning students, but if you are interested in how the development course has, well, developed, feel free to look around!

Color-coded Hints

I decided to do away with the solid gray hint boxes of previous years, instead replacing them with colorful boxes that can better portray their importance. Here is an example of each and what they mean:

This is a Warning. These are placed predominantly to let you know that you need to do another module before continuing.

This is a Checkpoint. These are places where you can check your progress to make sure you are on track.

This is an Alert. These are here to bring attention to common mistakes or subtle changes before they can cause issues.

This is Addition Info. These contain either more detail on a given topic or links to external resources.

This is a Tip. These are little tricks and thoughts from me to you, and are more for fun and inspiration than any actual important information.

New and Updated Assets

Beyond just new modules, I have further added more options for player colors, tiles, sound effects and even a new song! Furthermore I went back and updated many assets to both look and sound better, with especially egregious assets recieving a complete makeover.

Code Tweaks

As I have added more modules (and ones with greater complexity), I have needed to write more robust and flexible code. As such, returning students may need to look back to previous modules if newer ones have conflicts with older ones.

Site Updates

In addition to the hints, I have gone through and spiced up the site itself. I added more color, rewrote confusing sections for comprehension, and generally updated the visuals to be more polished. I hope you like the new look.
